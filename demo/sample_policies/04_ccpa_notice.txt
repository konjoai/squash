NOTICE AT COLLECTION FOR CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS

Effective: 2026-01-15

This notice describes the personal information that Acme Analytics
GmbH ("we", "us", "our") collects from California residents and the
purposes for which we collect it. It is provided in accordance with
the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 (CCPA), as amended by the
California Privacy Rights Act of 2020 (CPRA).

1. CATEGORIES OF PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  - Identifiers: name, email address, account ID, IP address.
  - Commercial information: subscription plan, billing history.
  - Internet or electronic network activity: pages visited, referrers,
    session timestamps, search queries within the dashboard.
  - Geolocation data: approximate location derived from IP address.
  - Inferences: usage patterns and engagement scores.

We do not collect biometric information, sensitive personal information
as defined by the CPRA, or precise geolocation.

2. PURPOSES FOR COLLECTION

We collect the categories listed above to: (a) provide and operate
the Service; (b) bill you and prevent fraud; (c) improve our Service
through aggregated analytics; (d) comply with legal obligations.

3. SALE AND SHARING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

We do not sell personal information for monetary consideration. We do
not share personal information with third parties for cross-context
behavioural advertising. You have the right to opt out of any future
sale or sharing — to exercise it, follow the "Do Not Sell or Share My
Personal Information" link in the footer of our website.

4. YOUR CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

As a California resident, you have the following rights:

  - Right to know what personal information we have collected about
    you, including categories and specific pieces.
  - Right to delete personal information we have collected from you,
    subject to certain statutory exceptions.
  - Right to correct inaccurate personal information we hold.
  - Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information.
  - Right to limit the use of sensitive personal information.

5. HOW TO EXERCISE YOUR RIGHTS

You may submit a verifiable request by emailing privacy@acme-analytics.example
or by calling 1-800-555-0199. We will verify your identity using the
information already on file before responding. We will respond within
the 45-day period required by the CCPA (extendable once by 45 days
when reasonably necessary).

6. NON-DISCRIMINATION

We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA
rights. We will not deny you the Service, charge you a different
price, or provide a different level or quality of service because you
exercised your rights.

7. AUTHORIZED AGENTS

You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf.
We may require proof of the agent's authority and may verify your
identity directly.

8. CONTACT

Questions about this notice or our privacy practices: privacy@acme-analytics.example.
