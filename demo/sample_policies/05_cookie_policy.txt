COOKIE POLICY

Last reviewed: 2026-01-15

This page explains how Acme Analytics uses cookies and similar tracking
technologies on our website.

1. WHAT IS A COOKIE?

A cookie is a small text file that a website saves to your device when
you visit. We also use local storage and web beacons; these are
covered by this policy alongside cookies.

2. COOKIES WE USE

  Strictly necessary
    - session       Keeps you signed in.                       (1 hour)
    - csrf          Cross-site request forgery defence.        (session)
    - eu_consent    Records your cookie preference.            (12 months)

  Functional
    - theme         Remembers light or dark mode.              (12 months)
    - locale        Remembers your language preference.        (12 months)

  Analytics
    - _sq_aid       First-party analytics, anonymised.         (90 days)
    - _sq_path      Records the page you came from.            (session)

We do not use any advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

3. HOW TO MANAGE YOUR COOKIE PREFERENCES

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies via the consent banner
shown the first time you visit, or at any time via the "Cookie
Settings" link in the footer of our website. You can also block or
delete cookies through your browser settings; doing so may affect
your ability to use parts of our service.
