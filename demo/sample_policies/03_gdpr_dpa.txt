DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the master service
agreement between the Customer (the "Controller") and Acme Analytics
GmbH (the "Processor"). It is entered into pursuant to Article 28 of
Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (the "GDPR").

1. DEFINITIONS

Terms used but not defined here have the meaning given to them in the
GDPR. The Data Controller, Data Processor, Data Subject, Personal Data,
and Processing roles are as defined in the GDPR.

2. SCOPE AND PURPOSE OF PROCESSING

The Processor will process personal data only on the documented
instructions of the Controller, including with regard to international
transfers. The categories of data subjects, categories of personal
data, the nature and purpose of processing, and the duration of
processing are described in Annex A.

3. LAWFUL BASIS

The Controller represents that it has identified an appropriate lawful
basis under Article 6 (and where applicable, Article 9) of the GDPR
for each processing activity described in Annex A.

4. SUB-PROCESSORS

The Controller authorises the Processor to engage the sub-processors
listed in Annex B. The Processor will give the Controller 30 days'
notice of any intended addition or replacement of a sub-processor and
will impose the same data-protection obligations on every
sub-processor by written contract.

5. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

The Processor will assist the Controller, by appropriate technical and
organisational measures, in fulfilling the Controller's obligation to
respond to requests by data subjects exercising their rights under
Articles 15 to 22 of the GDPR (right of access, right to rectification,
right to erasure, right to data portability, right to restrict
processing, right to object).

6. SECURITY OF PROCESSING

The Processor will implement the technical and organisational measures
described in Annex C, including encryption in transit and at rest,
access control, network segmentation, key management, and continuous
monitoring. Annex C will be updated at least annually.

7. PERSONAL DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION

The Processor will notify the Controller of any personal data breach
without undue delay and in any event within 24 hours of becoming aware
of it, providing all information reasonably available to support the
Controller's notification to the supervisory authority within the 72
hours required by Article 33 of the GDPR.

8. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Where the Processor transfers personal data to a third country or
international organisation, it will rely on the Standard Contractual
Clauses (Module Two: Controller to Processor) approved by Commission
Implementing Decision (EU) 2021/914, supplemented by a documented
transfer impact assessment and any additional safeguards required.

9. AUDIT

The Controller (or an independent auditor mandated by the Controller)
may, on reasonable notice, audit the Processor's compliance with this
DPA, no more than once per calendar year except in the event of a
personal data breach.

10. RETENTION AND DELETION

On termination of the master service agreement, the Processor will,
at the Controller's choice, delete or return all personal data processed
on the Controller's behalf, and delete all existing copies, unless
storage is required by EU or member-state law.

11. DATA PROTECTION OFFICER

Both parties have appointed a Data Protection Officer reachable at
dpo@acme-analytics.example and at the contact named in Annex A
respectively.

12. GOVERNING LAW

This DPA is governed by the laws of Germany. The competent courts of
Berlin have exclusive jurisdiction.
