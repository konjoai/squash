Acme AI — Data Protection Policy (GDPR-aligned)

1. Lawful basis. We process personal data only where we have a lawful basis under
Article 6 of the EU General Data Protection Regulation: consent, contract,
legitimate interest, or legal obligation. The basis for each processing activity
is recorded in our Article 30 Record of Processing Activities.

2. User rights. Data subjects have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict,
port, and object to the processing of their personal data. Requests are
acknowledged within 72 hours and fulfilled within 30 days at no cost. A Data
Protection Officer is appointed and reachable at dpo@acme.example.

3. Data minimisation. We collect only the personal data strictly necessary for
the purpose disclosed at collection. Pseudonymisation is applied to identifiers
in analytics pipelines. Raw event logs are retained for 90 days, then deleted.

4. Security. All personal data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit
with TLS 1.3. Access is governed by role-based access control with quarterly
audits. Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all administrative access.

5. Breach notification. Personal data breaches are reported to the supervisory
authority within 72 hours and to affected data subjects without undue delay
where the breach is likely to result in high risk to their rights and freedoms.

6. International transfers. Transfers outside the EEA are made only under
Standard Contractual Clauses or to jurisdictions with an adequacy decision.

7. Retention. Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the
disclosed purpose. The retention schedule is reviewed annually.
