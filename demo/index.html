      09. Why this matters

      Three questions every team shipping AI eventually has to answer.

      📚

      Was this model trained on copyrighted data?

      Squash gives you a signed report that walks back through the family tree to the original training corpus.

      🛡️

      Can I prove this AI hasn't been tampered with?

      Cryptographic attestations bind the model bytes to a signature. If anything changed, verification fails loudly.

      🎯

      Did this output come from the model I think it did?

      Every attestation contains the exact SHA-256 of the model that produced it.
